About Basket Bros

- Basket Bros presents a side-scrolling basketball experience where you face formidable opponents. Engage in this intense basketball game, soaring through the air and executing powerful slams to secure victory!

BasketBros, crafted by Blue Wizard Digital, offers a lively two-player basketball game for weekend leisure. Select your character, engage in one-on-one battles, and unlock new characters and features after victories. Customize uniforms and enjoy the thrill of scoring points against your opponent for a dynamic and entertaining gaming experience.

- Developer

Players can choose from a variety of characters, each with unique abilities and skills, and compete in different arenas. Overall, "Basket Bros" offers a fun and competitive gaming experience for basketball fans to enjoy online.
